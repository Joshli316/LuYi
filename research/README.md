## Key Findings

- **H-1B selection rate** has dropped to ~15-25% depending on wage level under the new weighted lottery (FY2027)
- **O-1A** is viable for more students than most realize — 3 of 8 criteria threshold is achievable with planning
- **EB-2 NIW** approval rates dropped from ~91% (FY2022) to ~36% (Q4 FY2025) due to volume surge and policy shifts
- **India EB-2 backlog** is 12+ years; Cato projects 134 years for new filers — NIW/EB-1 upgrades are essential
- **PERM** takes 22-26 months end-to-end, making early employer engagement critical
- **STEM OPT** is under active threat from 5+ policy directions but no final rule yet
- **L-1A→EB-1C** is one of the fastest green card paths (~16 months for non-backlogged countries)
- **Immigration scams** are widespread — University of Farmington sting affected 600+ students
